With over 12 years of practice experience, Dim-Jamora Dermatology aspires to bring the highest quality of dermatologic care to individuals from all walks of life in the Philippines. Located at The Medical City Ortigas and Makati Medical Center, the Clinic is at the hub of a vibrant medical community. At the helm of Dim-Jamora Dermatology is Dr. Krisinda Dim-Jamora, the first Philippine member of the American College of Mohs Surgery and a Philippine Board-Certified Dermatologist.
